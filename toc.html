About me

I am a student in Computer Science, 3rd year at Lille University, Science and Technology Faculty. I love programming, and I am very interested by OOP aspects, design patterns, and mobile android programming. I also participate to open source projects in the Pharo community as Pillar, etc. You ca find my CV here. I appreciate working with people. So if you are interested to work with me, contact me.

I'am also interested by reading, sport and travelling.

Projects

Hi, the main projects on those I am working are :

  • Pharo : an object-oriented language. I'am contributing by proposing some fix to bugs found in the implementation.
  • Pillar : a Pharo tool used to generate documents in different formats Pdf, Html, Epub. With Pillar, we wrote a document one time in pillar syntax and generate as you want. It provides a list of mustache

templates which can be used to design our documents.

  • Ecstatic : an extension of Pillar just for generating web sites. It is a small tool with can serve static html pages.
  • DressCode : mobile application for showing designs
